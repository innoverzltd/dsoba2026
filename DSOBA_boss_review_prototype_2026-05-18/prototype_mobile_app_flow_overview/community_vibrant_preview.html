About the dinner

The annual dinner is the highlight of the year, fostering Old Boys' connections and supporting the school's continued development.

Prize requirements
  • Raffle prize minimum value: HKD 3,000.
  • Table prize minimum value: HKD 150, minimum 150 pieces.
  • Door prize has no minimum value requirement.
Sponsor recognition

Prize sponsors may be recognised on raffle tickets, event backdrop and the Annual Dinner booklet, subject to organising committee approval.
